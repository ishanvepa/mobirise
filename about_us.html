About Us 

Young people addressing issues through media productions. 

Our Documentary on Youth Poverty in Tallahassee 

Our team has produced documentaries on issues such as Youth Poverty in Tallahassee and the legacy of the Voting Rights Act of 1965. Our work has reached thousands through multiple features in the press and at events such as Whole Child Leon's 2020 Summit on Children where our documentary on Youth Poverty in Tallahassee was viewed by prominent leaders across the state of Florida, including members of the state legislature. Our work has also resulted in thousands of dollars raised for charitable causes.
