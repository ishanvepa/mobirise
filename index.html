    Recognition and Impact 

    Recognition Youth Entrepreneurship Expo

    Our team received third place at  the Soul of Southside Youth Entrepreneurship Competition. Our exhibit was  presented to city commissioners, school board members, and state senators. Our team received recognition for the social impact made by our projects. 

    $1000 Raised for Families Affected by COVID-19

    During the COVID-19 Pandemic, we started a GoFundMe for Tallahassee families impacted by COVID-19. The money our team raised went towards toys, food items, and books. 

    2020 Summit on Children 

    The 2020 Summit on Children convened leaders across the state of Florida. Our documentary on Youth Poverty in Tallahassee was featured at this event and was viewed by hundreds including members of the Florida state legislature.
